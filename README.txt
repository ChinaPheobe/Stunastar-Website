StunAstar Online Store

Description
Sports Outfit Online Store is a web-based platform where users can purchase sports outfits and accessories. Whether you're into running, yoga, or team sports, our store offers a wide range of high-quality athletic wear to meet your needs.

Features
- Browse and search for sports outfits by category or keyword
- View detailed product descriptions and images
- Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout

 Usage
1. Browse the different categories or use the search bar to find specific sports outfits.
2. Click on a product to view more details, such as size options and pricing.
3. Add items to your cart by selecting the desired quantity and clicking "Add to Cart".
4. Review your cart and proceed to checkout when ready.
5. If you're a new customer, you can create an account during checkout to save your information for future purchases.

 Technologies Used
- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
